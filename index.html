UW-Madison Calendar Converter

This site converts UW-Madison course schedules into something you can import into multiple online calendars.

Convert

How to use

1. Navigate to your MyUW home page and click the Course Schedule tab.

2. Locate the text underneath the calendar, and copy it all to your clipboard.

3. On this page, paste the text into the textbox on the left. Select the range of dates you want the entries to populate on your calendar (Typically the start and end of the semester) and click convert!

4. Import the returned file into Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, etc. You should your class schedule on your calendar now!
